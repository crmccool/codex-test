CSV cleaning assumptions used
  • Data columns are read directly from the CSV headers: name, primary_department, countries_normalized, regions_derived, and focus_areas.
  • countries_normalized and regions_derived values are split on commas into separate tokens.
  • The geography filter matches against both country tokens and region tokens in one list.
